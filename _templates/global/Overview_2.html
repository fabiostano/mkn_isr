You will receive information about each decision alternative on different criteria. Please note that out of the given alternatives there is only one best decision based on these criteria. An alternative gets chosen if the majority (at least 2 out of the 3 team members) selects a certain alternative.

Here is how the scoring system works:

  • Teams who choose the best alternative will score 15 Points.
  • Teams who choose a suboptimal alternative will score 10 Points.
  • Teams who choose the worst alternative will score 5 Points.
  • Teams with no clear majority will score 0 Points.
