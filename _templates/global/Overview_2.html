Please note that out of the given project alternatives there is only one best decision based on these criteria. A project gets chosen if the majority (at least 2 out of the 3 team members) select it.

Here is how the scoring system works:

  • Teams who choose the best project will score 15 Points.
  • Teams who choose a suboptimal project will score 10 Points.
  • Teams who choose the worst project will score 5 Points.
  • Teams with no clear majority will score 0 Points.
