You will receive information about each project on different criteria. Please note that out of the three projects there is only one best, second best and worst project based on these criteria. A project gets chosen if the majority (at least 2 out of the 3 team members) selects a certain project.

Here is how the scoring system works:

  • Teams who choose the best project will score 15 Points .
  • Teams who choose the second-best project will score 10 Points .
  • Teams who choose the worst project will score 5 Points .
  • Teams with no clear majority will score 0 Points .
