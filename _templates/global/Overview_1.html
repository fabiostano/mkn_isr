Here is how the study works:

  • You and your team-members will receive information about three projects. Only one of them is the best for your company!
  • To make an informed decision, you must collaborate and share the information you have with your team. Please note that the project information has been distributed between you and your team members.
  • Your objective is to communicate effectively to compile an overview of all project information, and choose the best project.
  • You will have 5 minutes to study the project information and then a {% block discussion_mode %}7-minute discussion{% endblock %} before the project selection phase. Use this time wisely to share, discuss and strategize with your team.
  • At the end of the discussion you will have to vote for your preferred project. The project with the highest number of votes will be your team's choice.
