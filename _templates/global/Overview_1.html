Here is how the task series works:

  • In three rounds, you and your team-members will make informed management decisions together. In each case, only one of them is the best for your company!
  • To make an informed decision, you must collaborate and share the information you have with your team. Please note that the necessary information has been distributed between you and your team members.
  • Your objective is to communicate effectively to compile an overview of all the given information, and choose the best project.
  • You will have 3 minutes to study the given information and then a {% block discussion_mode %} 5-minute chat discussion{% endblock %}. Use this time wisely to share, discuss and strategize with your team.
  • After the discussion you will have to vote for your preferred decision. The project with the highest number of votes will be your team's choice.
