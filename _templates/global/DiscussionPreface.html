Understanding Sentiment Analysis

During your team discussion, you will see a real-time sentiment analysis graph below the meeting window. This tool uses artificial intelligence to analyze the emotional tone of the conversation.

How to Read the Sentiment Graph:

  • The graph shows sentiment scores ranging from -1 to +1
  • The line changes color based on the conversation's tone
  • Updates occur automatically as your team discusses
Positive Sentiment

(0.05 to 1.0)

Shows in Green

Indicates constructive, supportive discussion

Neutral Sentiment

(-0.05 to 0.05)

Shows in Orange

Indicates factual, objective discussion

Negative Sentiment

(-1.0 to -0.05)

Shows in Red

Indicates tension or disagreement

Why This Matters: The sentiment graph can help your team monitor the tone of your discussion and maintain a constructive dialogue while making important decisions.
